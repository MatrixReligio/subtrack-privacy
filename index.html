Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026

SubTrack is developed by MatrixReligio. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data SubTrack collects and how it is used.

Data Storage

All your subscription data, categories, payment history, and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data belongs to you.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, SubTrack uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. We have no access to your iCloud data. For details, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.
  • We do not collect crash reports or usage data.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Network Requests

SubTrack makes the following network requests:

  • Currency exchange rates: The app fetches exchange rate data from FloatRates, a publicly available currency rate service. No personal data is sent in these requests.
  • iCloud sync: Data synchronization through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure (if iCloud is enabled).
  • In-App Purchases: Processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

On-Device AI Features

SubTrack Pro includes AI-powered spending analysis features. All analysis is performed entirely on your device. No subscription data, income information, or analysis results are ever transmitted to any server.

Optional Monthly Income

You may optionally provide a monthly income reference to calculate spending health scores. This value is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Notifications

SubTrack sends local notifications for subscription renewal reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or push notification services.

Widgets & Siri Shortcuts

SubTrack provides Home Screen widgets and Siri Shortcuts that display subscription data. These features access data from your device's local storage only.

Children's Privacy

SubTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter the subscription details (name, price, billing cycle, etc.), and tap Save.

How does iCloud sync work?
If iCloud is enabled on your device, SubTrack automatically syncs your subscriptions across all your Apple devices. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account on all devices.

What's included in Pro?
SubTrack Pro unlocks unlimited subscriptions (free version supports up to 5), CSV export, and AI-powered spending analysis. You can upgrade via a yearly subscription or a one-time lifetime purchase.

How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Export to CSV (Pro feature). This generates a CSV file with all your subscription data that you can share or save.

How do I restore my purchase?
In-app purchases are tied to your Apple Account and are restored automatically. If your Pro status is not showing, try restarting the app.

How do I delete all my data?
Go to Settings > Reset All Data. This permanently deletes all subscriptions, categories, and payment history. This action cannot be undone.

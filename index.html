SubTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Overview

SubTrack ("the App") is a subscription management app developed by MatrixReligio LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Storage

SubTrack stores all your subscription data, categories, and settings locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • Subscription data (names, prices, dates, categories) is stored on-device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud, your data is synced through Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Monthly income reference (optional): If you provide this, it is stored locally and used only to calculate a spending health score. It is never transmitted.

Exchange Rate Data

The App fetches currency exchange rates from a third-party API (FloatRates) to support multi-currency conversion. Only the exchange rate data is downloaded; no personal information is sent to this service.

In-App Purchases

SubTrack offers optional Pro features via in-app purchases processed by Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Analytics and Tracking

SubTrack does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral analytics.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or social media SDKs.

Notifications

SubTrack may send local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription renewals. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Children's Privacy

SubTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
